### docker 

https://docs.docker.com/engine/install/ubuntu/

老版本的`docker`有三部分构成docker`, `docker.io`, or `docker-engine，如果有安装，先卸载

```sh
sudo apt-get remove docker docker-engine docker.io containerd runc
```
